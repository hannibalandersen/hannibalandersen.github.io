Free as in Vacant, Costless and Voluntary

Posted on facebook:

Dear friends!

I’m participating in ’The Final Exhibition’ at Pakhuset on Langelinie with an office space that you can use to work on your office related work. Maybe you have some applications to write, emails to answer, ‘job-logs’ to fill out – no matter what kind assignments you might have, you are welcome to come work at the office, for free.

In the office you will find modern interior, Wi-fi, coffee and tea, and you can see the ocean from the window. I will be there working myself and it would be lovely to have your company. Please share with your friends!

Book your free office workspace on +45 91416279.
Address: Langelinie allé 47, Copenhagen Ø
Opening hours: 12:00 - 17:00 (Thursday, 12:00 - 22:00)

The opening of the show is tomorrow, Friday the 25th of August, from 17 to 22 – Hope to see you there!

X,
